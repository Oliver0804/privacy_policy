BASHCAT Applications Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2025

Scope: This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications developed by BASHCAT, including but not limited to:

  • Wooden Fish App (Merit Machine Gun)
  • Other applications developed by BASHCAT

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the functionality of different applications, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Location Data: When you enable location sharing features in applications that support location services, we collect your geographical location data.
  • Usage Data: Including app usage frequency, feature usage statistics, and other usage data.
  • Device Data: Device model, operating system version, and other technical information to improve app performance.
  • Account Data: In applications that require account functionality, we may collect basic information such as username and email address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:

  • Providing core functionality of each application
  • Displaying community features in supported applications (only with user consent)
  • Analyzing and improving our services based on usage statistics
  • Providing technical support and customer service
  • Sending important service notifications and updates

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. In certain applications, your data may be shared in the following ways:

  • Anonymously displayed in community features within the app, with your explicit consent
  • With third-party service providers necessary for service delivery (such as cloud storage, analytics services)
  • With relevant authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We employ appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Using encryption technology to protect data transmission
  • Regularly updating security measures
  • Restricting employee access to personal information
  • Regular backup and monitoring of system security

5. Data Retention

We retain your data until:

  • You request account deletion
  • The minimum retention period required by law ends
  • A reasonable period after achieving the collection purpose

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access Right: Request to view the data we hold about you
  • Correction Right: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion Right: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction Right: Request restriction of processing your data in certain circumstances
  • Withdrawal Right: Withdraw consent for location sharing at any time

7. Location Services

In applications that support location services:

  • Location sharing features are entirely based on your voluntary choice
  • You can disable location sharing in application settings at any time
  • Disabling location sharing does not affect other features of the application
  • Location data is only collected and used with your consent
  • Location data from different applications is not shared between applications

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal data from children, we will immediately delete such data.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you within the relevant applications. We recommend that you periodically review this page to stay informed of any changes.
